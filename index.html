NICOLA LERRA

Biography

Nicola Lerra is a composer and keyboard synthesizer player, founder of the Film Music Score company, a group of composers chosen by him to create soundtracks for film products. His works are distinguished by the integration of electronic music sound with traditional orchestral arrangements. At the age of twenty-five, he is the first and only young Italian winner of the prestigious Hollywood Music in Media Awards, an award that honors professional composers and musicians who work in the film industry for audiovisual products such as films, trailers, TV series, commercials. Guests and candidates present at the evening: Alexandre Desplat, Justin Hurwitz, Alan Silvestri, Hans Zimmer . Among the most important and well-known productions are: " The New Era (From “the New Era”) " soundtrack composed and dedicated to the Final Trailer of the Marvel movie "The Amazing Spider-man 2 - The Power of Electro" with more than a million listen to the music and video platforms, another production has been the animated short film "My Grandfather Was a Cherry Tree". This product it was considered and judged as a masterpiece by international festivals and by the most prestigious juries among which Sofia Coppola, Julienne Moore and it was received a nominee on the list of seventy best short films in the Oscar 2017. He recently played for the Nobel Peace Prize, Prof. Muhammad Yunus, at the ceremony for the delivery of the "lamp of peace".
